Who we are ?

Vedak is a platform to connect to an exclusive pool of business consultants, software engineers and designers (web and graphic) for enterprise projects.

As a team, we believe and see that the world of work around us is changing from employment to projects. Businesses are looking to hire people with specialist skills who can add value from day one.

We have faced problems of hiring right consultants and coders in the past and felt the need of a marketplace where you can be assured of best quality consultants, coders and designers. To ensure quality, we have a rigorous on-boarding process which emphasises the candidate work experience at leading companies, education from top colleges and individual interview to check communication and domain knowledge
